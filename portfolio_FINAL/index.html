MAKE JAZZ, NOT WAR! POSTER

NATIONAL STUDENT SHOW AND CONFERENCE: GENERAL DESIGN WINNER

This poster was created for the 18th International Festival, “Jazz in the Ruins” located in Gliwice, Poland. The theme of the 2022 Festival was “Make Jazz, Not War!”, in response to the war happening in the neighboring country of Ukraine.

PERSONAL STATIONARY

AMERICAN ADVERTISING AWARD RECIPIENT

My design was based around the letter K I hand-built, with the assimilated element of an eye at the top arm of the K. The idea was to create a fun and dynamic design that would stand out alongside other personal stationaries, I wanted it to be professional and unique, and personal to me.

GET OUT THE VOTE! POSTER

This poster was created for “Get out the Vote!”, an AIGA campaign. The goal was to encourage civic engagement by registering and turning out for voting in 2022 in the midterm and local elections.

FUTURE COMMERCE ILLUSTRATIONS

These illustrative assets were created for The Archetypes Creative Journal for Future Commerce. The journal explores the twelve archetypal characters most commonly found in literature: the innocent, the everyman, the explorer, the sage, the ruler, the outlaw, the caregiver, the creator, the jester, the magician, the lover, and the hero.

MASS BIO EDUCATION POSTER

This poster was created for Massachusetts Biotechnology Education Foundation to be displayed in Boston Public Schools. The concept was to create a colorful and diverse poster to help encourage kids to start a career in science.

ABOUT THE ARTIST!!!

Hello! Welcome to my website! My name is Katherine ter Kuile, I am a graphic designer and Illustrator based in Springfield, MO. I am currently finishing my BFA in Graphic Design, with Minors in Art History and Creative Writing. This summer of 2023 I will be interning at the Museum of fine Arts in Boston working in Exhibition Design. I love playing with different mediums and incorporating those into my design and illustration work. If you like minimal designs with playful colors and mixed media, let’s work together! Please, email me with any questions and inquiries, I look forward to working together!
